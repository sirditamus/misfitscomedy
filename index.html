Misfits' Comedy: Where Laughter Lives

Welcome to Misfits' Comedy, the city's premier destination for uproarious laughter and unforgettable nights. We're dedicated to showcasing the freshest and funniest comedic talent, creating a vibrant space where humor takes center stage.

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate comedy aficionados, Misfits' Comedy was born from a shared dream: to build a club that champions raw, authentic, and boundary-pushing stand-up. Our stage has since become a launchpad for stars and a haven for comedy lovers.

What sets us apart? It's our electric atmosphere, our commitment to diverse voices, and our uncanny ability to curate shows that hit every comedic note. Come experience the Misfits' magic – a place where every night is a celebration of wit and mirth.

Got Qs?

What's the show schedule?

We have shows every Friday and Saturday night, with surprise pop-up shows during the week. Check our schedule for the latest updates!

Can I reserve seats?

Yes, advance tickets are recommended as shows often sell out. You can reserve your seats directly through our website.

Is there a drink minimum?

Yes, there is a two-drink minimum per person for all shows. We have a full bar with a wide selection of drinks.

What's the dress code?

Our dress code is casual. Come as you are, but we recommend dressing for a fun night out!

Do you have parking?

Street parking is available, and there are several paid lots nearby. We also encourage ride-sharing services.
